Executive Summary

Russell Ivan Putnam is a documented lineal descendant of Roque Jacinto de Cota (c. 1725–1798), a Spanish‑era soldier associated with the founding‑era Los Angeles region and the Mission San Gabriel historical sphere.

Through Roque Jacinto de Cota’s daughter María Celia Bonifacia Cota Feliz (1758–1847), this lineage continues through the Feliz Californios and descendants whose lives span the Spanish, Mexican, and American periods of California history.

This site is organized as a clear lineage outline, historical context, and an indexed archive of supporting documentation.
